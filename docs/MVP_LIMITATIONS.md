# MVP Limitations

- Tee times are simulated and not real reservations.
- No booking fee or tee-time markup is collected.
- Handicap calculations are Match Play estimates unless manually entered as unverified official values.
- GHIN, RevenueCat, Apple, Google, EAS, push notifications, and real tee-time providers require future credentials.
- Seeded courses, golfers, messages, rounds, and leaderboards are fictional demo data.
- Location discovery uses generalized location and approximate distance.
- Legal review is still required for privacy, terms, age gating, consumer subscriptions, and location-based social features.
